About Me

Hi, I'm Mario. Currently, I'm an international undergraduate student at UCLA, double majoring in Computational Linguistics and Psychology.

My interests include -- but are not limited to: Reinforcement Learning, NLP, optimization (in coding but also overall in life), learning languages, art, philosophy, and Effective Altruism.
