The Black Box Project

A collaborative project with Redwood Research. I'm performing influence studies and adversarial experiments to find quantitative differences between models and their natural language inference. Overall, the black box project aims to analyze language models to elucidate their behavior at a higher level of abstraction.* Currently, we're trying to understand GPT2 small's Indirect Object Identification (link)

Past works:

    GPT3 and GPT neo's knowledge on syntax and semantics. (link)

* there are varying levels of abstraction for model analysis. They all provide different kinds of insights on the model but none of them are all-encompassing. In this project, we consider our language model as a probability distribution and decision function P(y|x). This is called behavioral study as opposed to technical interpretability.
