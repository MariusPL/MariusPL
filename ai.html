Transformer Black Box Investigation

Collaborating with Buck Shlegeris from Redwood Research, we are trying to find explanations and quantitative differences between the sizes of language models and their performance at next token prediction. Overall, the black box project aims to elucidate transformer and NN behavior, drawing connections to human cognosis and psychology.
