Features

  • Seed system (pseudo randomization).
  • Big Room Formation: adjacent rooms may combine into a singular bigger room.
  • Variable Rooms: nested classes allow each room to be randomly chosen from its corresponding room type, resulting in variation at low cost.
  • Smart Trail Generation: no dead ends or inaccessible rooms, no extremely long hallways. when two lanes cross, an intersection is created.
  • Optimized: no overlapping rooms or unecessary computations, lightweight and fast with linked list.
  • Important Rooms: multiple special rooms can be created at the end of each trail.
